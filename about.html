Providing a network for businesses to transform the world

SGAN (Singapore Angel Network Pte. Ltd) is the angel investment arm of the Thakral Group of Companies, headquartered in Singapore. Our portfolio consists of investment in a plethora of companies across the entire economic spectrum ranging from e-commerce to eco-tourism. We are constantly seeking new opportunities for investment and our objective is to partner with innovative businesses and individuals with disruptive business ideas and a strong desire to change the world.

As an entrepreneur with a revolutionary business idea, partnering with SGAN will not only boost you financially, but it will also equip you with cutting-edge strategic investment from seasoned business personnel who are looking to mentor startups such as yours.

Furthermore, we are eager to maintain our relationship with you beyond the angel stage (where we typically invest upto $25-50K) and provide subsequent funding as and when your business becomes an established and successful entity.

SGAN operates as a subsidiary of the Thakral Group of Companies, a multi-billion dollar conglomerate currently operating in 36 countries with 20,000 employees.
The group’s operational breadth covers a wide range of activities from distribution and retail of Consumer Electronics, Computers & Peripherals, IT services, Real Estate Development, Hospitality, Logistics, Manufacturing of Yarn, export and retail of branded garments and accessories.

As an SGAN investee, you gain access to the Group’s unmatched network & management expertise to help connect your business to the world.
